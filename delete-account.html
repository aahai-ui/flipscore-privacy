Account Deletion Request

FlipScore — last updated April 27, 2026

You can request deletion of your FlipScore account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address below.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to flip.score.app@gmail.com with the subject line Account Deletion Request. Please include the email address associated with your FlipScore account in the message body.

Email Deletion Request

What Gets Deleted

Upon receiving your request, the following data will be permanently deleted within 30 days:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • All scan history and analysis results
  • All inventory records including item details, purchase prices, and sale data
  • Your subscription usage counters and preferences
⚠️ This action is irreversible. Deleted data cannot be recovered. We recommend exporting your inventory data via the CSV export feature before submitting a deletion request.

Data We Do Not Delete

Anonymous, non-identifiable analytics data (such as aggregated usage patterns) may be retained as it cannot be linked back to your identity.

Subscription

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your Google Play subscription. Please cancel your subscription through the Google Play Store before or after submitting your deletion request to avoid future charges.

Questions? Contact us at flip.score.app@gmail.com
